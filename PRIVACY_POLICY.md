# SplitTrip Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-03-30

## Summary
SplitTrip is a local-first trip expense app. Your trip data is stored on your device.

## Data We Process
- Trip content you create (trip names, participants, expenses, audit history).
- Optional backup files you export/import manually.

## Data Collection and Sharing
- We do not require account creation.
- We do not run a backend that receives your trip data.
- We do not sell personal data.
- We do not use advertising SDKs.
- We currently do not use third-party analytics.

## Backups and Sharing
You can export trip backups as JSON files. Export/share actions are initiated by you through your device's
share sheet. SplitTrip does not automatically upload backups.

## Data Retention
Data remains on your device until you delete it. Uninstalling the app or resetting the device removes local data.

## Security
We rely on the security controls provided by your device and operating system. Keep device backups and access
controls enabled.

## Children's Privacy
SplitTrip is not directed to children under 13.
